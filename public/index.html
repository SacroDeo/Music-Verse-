      How It Works
      Three simple steps
      1️⃣
      Search YouTube
      Type any song name or artist in the YouTube tab. Results load instantly.
      2️⃣
      Add to Playlist
      Hit the ➕ button on any result to queue it up. Or import a full Spotify playlist.
      3️⃣
      Download as MP3
      Click ⬇ on any track, or use Download All to batch-download your entire playlist.
